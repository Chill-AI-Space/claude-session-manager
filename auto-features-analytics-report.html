Key Findings & Recommendations

What works well

  • + 74.4% crash recovery rate — 3 out of 4 crashes auto-heal
  • + Haiku LLM filtering correctly skips 51.6% of stalls (Claude asking questions)
  • + 100% permission escalation success — all 13 escalations resolved
  • + ~40% of all interactions automated on peak days
  • + Late-night crash recovery — 105 crashes at 23h-2h recovered without human wake-up

What needs improvement

  • - 72.9% exit:null failures — most failed retries have no error info. Need better error capture
  • - gated-info: 197 stalls — MCP operations cause massive stall spikes. Consider timeout tuning
  • - Auto-retry disabled since Mar 13 — 28 unrecovered crashes in 2 days
  • - Rate limit (3/hour) may be too aggressive for crash-heavy sessions like job-creation
  • - Recovery time varies wildly — avg 45 min but some take hours (queue backlog?)

Bottom Line

Over 11 days, auto-features handled 430 interventions without human input — 374 crash recoveries, 43 stall continues, and 13 permission escalations. That's roughly ~39 auto-interventions per day when enabled. The system is particularly valuable for late-night crashes (23h-2h) and for projects like job-creation and interview-engine where recovery rate exceeds 80%.

Recommendation: Re-enable auto-retry. The 74.4% success rate means 3 out of 4 crashes self-heal. Consider raising the rate limit from 3/hour to 5/hour for crash-heavy projects.
